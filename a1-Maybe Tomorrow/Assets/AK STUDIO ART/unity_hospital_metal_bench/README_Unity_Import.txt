Unity import:
1. Put the .obj and .mtl files into the same Unity Assets folder.
2. Select the .obj in Unity.
3. Recommended Scale Factor: 1.
4. Use the generated materials or replace them with Unity metallic materials.
5. Suitable for a hospital lobby, clinic waiting area, corridor, or reception scene.

Design notes:
- Based on the provided reference image: three connected perforated metal seats, silver tubular armrests, central support beam, angled metal legs, and round foot pads.
- The perforations are modeled as dark inset surface details for better game performance.
- Approximate real-world size: 4.1m wide, 1.6m high, 1.1m deep.
