Unity import:
1. Drag hospital_patient_bed.obj and hospital_patient_bed.mtl into the same Unity Assets folder.
2. Select the OBJ file.
3. Recommended Scale Factor: 1.
4. Use the included MTL materials or replace them with Unity Standard/URP materials.
5. For metallic parts, use Metallic 0.8-1.0 and Smoothness 0.4-0.7.
6. Suitable for a hospital ward, emergency room, clinic room, or patient room scene.

Design based on the uploaded image:
- White plastic headboard and footboard with handle slots.
- Light blue mattress.
- White pillow and folded sheet.
- Silver metal side rails and frame.
- Four caster wheels.
- Simple wall medical service rail included as an optional background prop.

Approximate scale:
- Length: 3.25 m
- Width: 1.65 m including side rails
- Height: 1.75 m including headboard
